Background

Hi! My name is Yvette Tuyambaze, and I am currently in my second year of a bachelor's degree in computing at African Leadership University. I am from from Rwanda's Western Province, Rubavu District.

Education history before coming to ALU

I studied Advanced Level at Lycée Notre Dame de Cîteaux - Kigali,Rwanda, and received a High School Certificate.

ALU Journey

I first learned about ALU when ALU alumni students from the Rwanda Campus visited my high school to inform us more about it and encourage us to apply.

I wanted to study at ALU because I believed that they adopt an innovative strategy in which students learn largely by practising in the actual world.

My application process, I applied to pursue Bachelor's of Computing in Mauritius and was admitted, which excited me.

My advice to prospective students is to set high personal and intellectual expectations for themselves and to live up to them.

Experience in Mauritius
