  • Hi, I'm Funke

    My name is Funke Esther Aderoju. I am from Nigeria, my hometown is Ikire, Osun State. I studied at Abraham's Seeds College, Nigeria, where I write wrote my high school leaving certificate (WAEC).

  • ALU Journey

    I heard about ALU through Patoranking a Nigerian artiste, after doing thorough research I realized ALU is my dream school because of the structure and their vision and mission which aligns with my value.

    The application process was quite complicated and the fact it was during the pandemic which made it difficult and been accepted was the best thing that happened to me in the year 2020, moving from Nigeria to another country for the first time felt unreal and a dream come true to me, meeting other students was awesome and interesting.

    So far the classes have been amazing, and I have been able to work build a website from scratch I also plan to open a website for my business. My advice to prospective students is to be determined and consistent in whatever they do. My time at ALU spent so far has been amazing from the classes, working and earning money as a hairstylist, and getting to meet amazing customers is the best feeling.
